About the club

A running club for every kind of runner.

Carolina Running Club is built in Alba Iulia for people who want to move, meet new friends, and enjoy the Cetate together. Whether you are chasing a PR, getting back into running, or showing up for your first group run, you belong here. No pace limits. No gatekeeping. Just good runs and better company.

01

All paces welcome

Run, jog, walk-run, or cruise. Nobody needs to prove they are fast to be part of the group. Your pace is the right pace.

Alergi în ritmul tău. Important este să vii.

02

Community first

The post-run smiles, photos, coffee, and conversation are just as important as the kilometers. We run together, celebrate together.

03

Consistency over perfection

Show up when you can, build momentum, and let the club help make running feel sustainable and fun. Long-term over one-time heroes.

How it works

Three simple steps to your first Carolina run.

↗

Show up

Check Instagram or the website for the latest details, then arrive a few minutes early. We always have space for one more runner.

⌁

Choose your pace

Pick a distance and settle into a pace that feels good. The goal is movement, joy, and community—not speed records.

☕

Stay after

Hang out before and after the run for photos, recovery chats, new friendships, and coffee at Framm's. This is where the magic happens.

FAQ

Questions before you lace up?

Do I need to be fast? / Trebuie să fiu rapid?

No. Carolina Running Club is open to all paces. We care more about showing up and having fun than running a specific speed. Walk, jog, or sprint—you're welcome.

Nu. Toate ritmurile sunt binevenite. Cel mai important este să apari și să alergi în ritmul tău.

Is it free? / Este gratuit?

Yes. Regular community runs are completely free. We believe running should bring people together, not separate them by cost.

Da. Alergările obișnuite sunt gratuite.

Do I need to register? / Trebuie să mă înscriu?

For most runs, just show up. Main details are posted on Instagram, and the WhatsApp group is private—ask us via Instagram DM after your first run and we'll add you.

Pentru alergările obișnuite, poți veni direct. Detaliile sunt pe Instagram, iar grupul de WhatsApp se primește prin DM.

What distance do you run? / Ce distanță alergăm?

We usually run 1 or 2 loops of Șanțurile Cetății: about 3 km or 6 km. You can choose before the run starts.

De obicei alergăm 1 sau 2 ture: aproximativ 3 km sau 6 km.

What should I bring? / Ce să aduc?

Wear comfortable shoes, bring water if you want it, and arrive a few minutes early. Everything else is optional. We'll take care of the community vibe.

Încălțăminte comodă, apă dacă vrei și câteva minute în plus pentru notiți despre traseu.

Can I bring a friend? / Pot aduce un prieten?

Absolutely! We love new runners and your friends will too. Bring everyone you think might enjoy running in a welcoming community.
