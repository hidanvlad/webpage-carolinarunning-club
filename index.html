About the club

A running club for every kind of runner.

Carolina Running Club is built for people who want to move, meet new friends, and enjoy the miles together. Whether you are chasing a PR, getting back into running, or showing up for your first group run, you belong here.

01

All paces welcome

Run, jog, walk-run, or cruise. Nobody needs to prove they are fast to be part of the group.

02

Community first

The post-run smiles, photos, coffee, and conversation are just as important as the miles.

03

Consistency over perfection

Show up when you can, build momentum, and let the club help make running feel sustainable.

FAQ

Questions before you lace up?

Do I need to be fast?

No. Carolina Running Club is open to all paces. We care more about showing up than running a specific speed.

Is it free?

Yes. Regular community runs are free unless a special event says otherwise.

Do I need to register?

For most runs, just show up. If we host a special event, we will share an RSVP link on the website and Instagram.

What distance do you run?

Most social runs include 3-mile and 5-mile options, with longer routes added for Saturday meetups.

What should I bring?

Wear comfortable shoes, bring water if you want it, and arrive a few minutes early for route notes.
